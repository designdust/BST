Brief History Of Blaise Scientific Trading

Blaise-Scientific Trading is a local company engaged in the distribution of laboratory equipment and supplies primarily for Water and Environmental Analysis.

Exclusive Distributor of Palintest, Ltd. and United Chemical Technologies (UCT) in the Philippines which was started on June 16, 2016.

Registered in DTI as Sole Proprietorship.

Their existing customers are Government agencies such as Water Districts, Department of Health (DOH), Bureau of Fisheries & Aquatic Resources (BFAR), Department of Science and Technology (DOST), Laguna Lake Development Authority (LLDA), Public Hospitals, Academe and etc. For Private entities such as Concessionaires like Macro Asia, Manila Water, Maynilad, Prime Water Infrastructure, High Rise buildings, Private Hospitals and etc.

General Problem

How to develop a BST Connect as CRM System for Blaise-Scientific Trading that will help to improve the company’s interaction with current and potential customers.

Specific Problems

  • How to develop a module that will improve their marketing campaign to help in finding prospects.
  • How to develop a module that will automatically generate quotations.
  • How to develop a module that will track and manage sales orders of the clients.
  • How to develop a module that will handle deliveries.
  • How to develop a module that will verify payments.
  • How to develop a module that will generate reports.
  • How to develop a module that will manage and organize clients’ contact information.
  • How to develop a module that will manage stocks and monitor its level.
